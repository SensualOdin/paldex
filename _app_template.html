The chain finder does a breadth-first search over every species you can reach by breeding pairs from what you own, then reconstructs the shortest path to the target. Pathing is gender-agnostic (you can always obtain the needed gender); the Katress/Wixen gender rule is only surfaced on the final step if relevant. Self-only Pals (legendaries, tower bosses, uniques, collab Pals) are only reachable if you already own one.
